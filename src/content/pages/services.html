Roof Replacement

When It’s Time for a New Roof

If your roof is nearing the end of its lifespan, a full roof replacement can restore protection and improve your home's overall value. Our Duluth roofing team replaces worn, damaged, or outdated roofing systems with modern materials designed to perform in Northern Minnesota's demanding climate. A new roof not only improves protection against snow, rain, and wind—it can also enhance curb appeal and energy efficiency.

During a roof replacement project, we carefully remove the old roofing materials, inspect the underlying structure, and install a new system designed for long-term durability. We also ensure proper ventilation and flashing installation so your roof performs reliably year after year.

Signs your roof may need replacement:

  • Curling or missing shingles
  • Frequent leaks or water stains
  • Roof age approaching 20–30 years
  • Storm or wind damage
  • Moss or algae growth on shingles

Roof Inspections

Free Roof Inspections in Duluth, MN

Regular roof inspections are one of the easiest ways to prevent costly roofing problems. Our professional roof inspections help homeowners in Duluth and across Northern Minnesota identify early signs of wear, storm damage, and potential leak areas before they develop into major repairs.

During an inspection, we examine shingles, flashing, ventilation, and overall roof condition to ensure everything is functioning properly. If we find issues, we provide clear explanations and practical recommendations so you can make informed decisions about maintenance or repairs.

Routine inspections are especially important after major storms or before the winter season when heavy snow and ice can place additional stress on roofing systems.
